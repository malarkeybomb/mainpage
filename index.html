WELCOME TO
MALARKEYVILLE, USA!

Jack King has one dream: get to Jamaica. There's just one problem — he's broke, in debt, and living in the chaos magnet known as Malarkeyville, USA.

With his best friend Ace at his side, Jack dives headfirst into one bad plan after another, chasing quick money through the strangest corners of town. Every shortcut looks like salvation, every new opportunity comes with a catch, and every wild swing at success only pulls him deeper into the madness.

Somewhere between wrecking balls, bad decisions, weird jobs, and a vacation that absolutely does not go as planned, Jack learns that in Malarkeyville, every solution comes with a brand-new problem.

It's bros, bongs, beach dreams, and beautiful disasters all the way down.
